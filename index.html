Biography

Ning Ma is pursuing his Ph.D. degree from 2018.09 in Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University. Before 2018, he got his bachelor's degree at QDU (Qingdao University, China). His research interests are mainly in few-shot graph learning, domain adaptation and their applications for medical. He is also a primary reviewer for top-tier conferences, such as ICLR, NeurIPS, ECCV and AAAI.
