Biography

Ning Ma is pursuing his Ph.D. degree from 2018.09 in Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, advised by Dr. Jiajun Bu and Dr. Haishuai Wang. Before 2018, he got his bachelor's degree at QDU (Qingdao University, China). His research interests are mainly in graph representation learning, domain adaptation/generalization and their applications for infant healthcare, especially for the neurodevelopmental risk detection from infant movement videos. He is also a jounior reviewer for top-tier conferences, such as ICLR, NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV and AAAI.
